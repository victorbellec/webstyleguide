Grid:
We use a Grid that follows this pattern:
Desktop:
  • 80px margin on both sides
  • 12 columns
  • Gutter of 24px
Tablet:
  • 40px margin on both sides
  • 12 columns
  • Gutter of 24px
Mobile:
  • 16px margin on both sides
  • 12 columns
  • Gutter of 16px
